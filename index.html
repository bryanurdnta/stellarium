ABOUT STELLARIUM

Embark on a spellbinding journey with the Stellarium

Coven Tour, where magic and wonder await you at

every turn. This unique gathering of friendly, fun-loving

witches offers an enchanting experience for the whole

family. Witness a dazzling opening parade, be

awestruck by a live magic show, learn the secrets of

potion-making, listen to mystical tales, and dance

under the stars in this once-a-year magical

extravaganza. Join us for a night of laughter,

surprises, and unforgettable memories with the

Stellarium witches, where magic truly comes to life!

TOO MANY YEARS

He Stellarium Coven isn't just a magical experience; it's a journey through the

annals of time, connecting us with a legacy as old as magic itself. These witches,

renowned for their enchanting tour, have been a part of our world's tapestry since

time immemorial.

From the very dawn of civilization, there have been tales and whispers of wise

women, mystics, and enchanters who could commune with nature, cast spells,

and brew mysterious potions. The Stellarium Coven is the modern embodiment of

this ancient lineage. Throughout history, they have been the guardians of mystical

knowledge, passing down their skills and secrets through generations.

Their existence has been a constant, a thread weaving through the fabric of

countless cultures and epochs. In every age, they have adapted, learning and

evolving while always keeping the core of their magic intact. Today, the Stellarium

witches bring together the wisdom of the ages with a contemporary twist, making

their ancient arts accessible and entertaining for audiences of our time.

So, when you step into the world of the Stellarium Coven, you're not just

experiencing a night of magic and fun; you're connecting with a timeless tradition that

has dazzled and intrigued humanity since the dawn of our existence.

The Stellarium witches are more than just performers; they are the bearers of an age-old

legacy, inviting you to be a part of the magical continuum that is as eternal

as the stars themselves.

ACT 1

Our adventure begins with a magical parade under the stars. Stellarium

witches, with their pointy hats and shimmering capes, dance around a

giant steaming cauldron. But don't worry! The only thing they're cooking

up is laughter and surprises.

ACT 2

One of our witches, the wise old Willow, will regale you with tales of

witches from around the world. Stories full of adventure, mystery, and, of

course, plenty of magic. Perfect for listening around a magical bonfire.

ACT 3

To wrap things up, Stellarium witches will invite everyone to join in a

magical dance under the stars. It doesn't matter if you can't dance; here,

magic will move your feet.

And much more! With magical games, mystical riddles, and enchanted

snacks, the Stellarium Coven is a place for the whole family to have fun

and be amazed.

Remember, the Stellarium Coven only appears once a year, when the

moon is at its brightest! So don't miss this unique opportunity to experience

magic like never before. The witches of Stellarium are waiting to share

with you a night you'll never forget!
